Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2026

Welcome to True Occupation. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect and use your information when you use our platform and mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Professional Data: Resumes, skills, and work history you upload for job matching.
  • Employer Data: Company details and registration documents for verification.
  • Usage Data: Test scores, interview performance, and how you interact with the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to provide AI-powered job matching, process applications, and facilitate employer verifications. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time via your user dashboard.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@trueoccupation.com.
